CSE 3216: Project Proposal Template

1. Title
 A concise and descriptive title for the project. Also try to give a small and lucrative
naming of your project. 
2. Partner Information
 Give rolls, emails and names of your partners 
3. Introduction
 An introductory section that explains the purpose of the proposal and the project&#39;s
importance. Give motivating examples why this project will be useful in real life
scenarios. Give some examples to show where the design patterns might be required in
your project to improve the quality of the design/codebase. 
4. Project Objectives
 Enumerate Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound (SMART)
objectives of the project. 
5. Software Development Methodology
 Discuss which type of software development process you are thinking to execute
(E.g., Agile, waterfall, Spiral, etc.). Identify some possible scenarios based on your
project that you are thinking to encounter. 
6. Scope of the Project
- Identify the functional requirements of the project
- Identify the non-functional requirements of the project
- Identify the possible limitations/constraints of the project

7. Technical Details
- Specify which platform (Web/Mobile/Software) you are choosing to complete the
project

2
- Specify your stacks - Programming languages, frameworks or any supporting
entity that you are planning to use
- Identify any hardware(Device, Computation Power, etc.) or software
constraints(Third Party libraries, specific library versions, operating system
versions. etc.) that your project might require.

8. Timeline
 A possible timeline to complete various key milestones of your project. Try to
formulate the design thinking for at most 3 months. They should included - requirement
analysis phase, design phase, implementation phase, testing phase. 
9. Conclusion
 Some concluding remarks over the overall points that you have written in this
submission
