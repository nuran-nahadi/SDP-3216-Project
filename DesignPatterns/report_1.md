# Assignment 2: Pattern-based Refactoring

**Total Marks:** 15
**Deadline:** 12 November 2025

## Objective
This assignment focuses on improving your existing project architecture by applying appropriate software design patterns.

You will identify opportunities for refactoring within your system, justify your choices, and demonstrate how design patterns enhance modularity, maintainability, and scalability.

## Tasks

1.  **Identify and Justify Design Patterns (5 marks)**
    * Choose at least three relevant design patterns.
    * Explain their purpose and show UML diagrams.
    * Describe what problem each solves within your project context.

2.  **Refactor and Implement (7 marks)**
    * Modify your existing codebase to integrate these patterns.
    * Include before/after architecture illustrations.
    * Apply one pattern on the frontend and one on the backend.

3.  **Reflection and Testing (3 marks)**
    * Write a 1-page reflection explaining improvements and trade-offs.
    * Include test evidence to verify functional stability.

## Deliverables

1.  Report
2.  Public GitHub Repository link
3.  Testing Summary (screenshots or logs)
